Welcome to the Personal Spending Tracker

This software is designed to help you track your spending and manage your budget effectively. Set a limit for your budget, track your spending and see where your money is going.

Here are a few tips for you to get started.

  1. Set your budget limit: Go to the "Budgets" page and click on "Set Budget". Enter your budget limit, start date, and end date, and save it.
  2. Track your expenses: Go to the "Expenses" page and click on "Add Expense". Enter the details of your expenses, such as the date, category, and amount, and save the expense.
  3. View your spending history: Go to the "History" page to view a summary of your spending history and see how close you are to reaching your budget limit.

Start tracking your spending today and take control of your finances!

Welcome to the Personal Spending Tracker

This software is designed to help you track your spending and manage your budget effectively. Set a limit for your budget, track your spending and see where your money is going.

Here are a few tips for you to get started.

  1. To set your budget limit, navigate to the "Budgets" page and click on "Set Budget". From there, input your desired budget limit, select a timeframe, and save your changes.
  2. Your budget will be automatically refreshed with the same name and limit once the end date is reached.
  3. Track your expenses: Go to the "Expenses" page and click on "Add Spending". Enter the details of your expenses, such as the date, category, and amount, and save the expense.
  4. View your spending history: Go to the "History" page to view a summary of your spending history and see how close you are to reaching your budget limit.
  5. Chatbot: To better make use of the Chatbot. Please use some keywords hello,pst,budget,expense,track,saving,finance,bye and etc.
  6. Gravatar: If you want to set profile image. Please vist https://en.gravatar.com/.

Start tracking your spending today and take control of your finances!
